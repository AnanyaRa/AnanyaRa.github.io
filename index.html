Overview

Welcome! I am a first-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Genomics and Computational Biology at the University of Pennsylvania Perelman School of Medicine. Prior to UPenn, I completed my undergraduate studies at Yale College in Computer Science, with certificates in Global Health and Statistics.

I am interested in applying machine-learning methods to clinical and genomic data to improve how we predict and prevent under-diagnosed diseases. My first rotation is with Dr. Shefali Setia-Verma. I am excited to be investigating the mediating effect of endometriosis and polycystic ovarian syndrome on patients' genetic risk for later developing diseases such as type 2 diabetes, hypertension, and hyperlipidemia.
